### Description

Jarvis Helpdesk helps support teams manage conversations, tickets, and customer context in one place. Built for omnichannel operations, it combines human agents with AI assistance to improve response time and service quality.

Key features:

- Unified inbox for multiple support channels
- Ticket lifecycle management and assignment
- AI-assisted support workflows
- Customer profile and conversation history
- Team collaboration for faster resolution

Jarvis Helpdesk is designed for support teams that need speed, consistency, and visibility across channels.
